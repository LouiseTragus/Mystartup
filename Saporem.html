How much do you know about food allergies ? :)

For everyone, everytime : Food allergies are a serious concern, triggered when the immune system reacts to specific food proteins. Common allergens include peanuts, tree nuts, shellfish, fish, eggs, milk, soy, wheat, and sesame seeds. Even tiny amounts can cause reactions ranging from mild discomfort to life-threatening anaphylaxis. Symptoms may include hives, swelling, difficulty breathing, nausea, and vomiting. 











Managing food allergies requires careful label reading, communication with restaurant staff, and awareness of potential cross-contamination. Despite challenges, increased awareness has led to better labeling and allergen-free options. Medical guidance is essential for managing allergies effectively, including emergency response plans. Education, communication, and proactive management are crucial for living safely with food allergies.
